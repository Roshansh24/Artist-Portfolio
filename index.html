Description

Timeless and tasteful, or modern and messy, flowers demand to be examined. I use multi-layered wax paint to create highly textured, three-dimension works on canvas and paper. I'm inspired by the color, wildness and creativity of springtime. I also recreate wedding bouquets and other meaningful arrangements to capture these focal points forever. In this collection, I've focused on the wildflowers of Maine, often growing unencumbered and inconspicuously. But they still take my breath away. Forget the demure; these flowers burst with energy, life and texture, layer upon layer of evolution and growth. They challenge your expectations of the everyday.
